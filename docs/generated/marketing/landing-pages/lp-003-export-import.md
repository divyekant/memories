## Problem Section
- **Section headline:** Knowledge trapped in one place helps no one
- **Pain points:**
  - New machine? Your AI starts from scratch while you explain everything again
  - Teammate discovered a critical fix? It's stuck on their instance
  - Merging two knowledge bases? Prepare for duplicates and contradictions
  - Switching infrastructure? Your AI's context doesn't come along

## Solution Section
- **Section headline:** Portable, smart, and safe
- **Value props:**
  - **Selective Export:** Choose exactly what to transfer — by project, date range, or everything
  - **Smart Import:** Automatically skips duplicates and keeps the newer version of conflicting facts
  - **Built-in Safety:** Automatic backup before every import — restore with one command
  - **Namespace Flexibility:** Reorganize how knowledge is categorized during import

## How It Works
- **Section headline:** Three steps to portable AI memory
- **Steps:**
  1. **Export** — Select your memories and save to a portable file
  2. **Import** — Load into any instance with your chosen conflict strategy
  3. **Done** — Smart dedup merged cleanly, backup protects your data
